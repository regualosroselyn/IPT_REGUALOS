WELCOME TO MY PROFILE

    

ROSELYN VIDAL REGUALOS

         

    Music can feel calming because of its ability to regulate emotions and soothe the mind.Slow tempos,  gentle melodies, and soft harmonies can reduce stress and create a sense of peace.The rhythmic patterns  and familiar tones can help relax both the body and mind, encouraging a meditative or reflective state. Calm music often mimics natural sounds,like flowing water or rustling leaves, which further enhances its calming effect.

    

 Sunsets can feel peaceful and inspiring because they mark the transition between day and night,  often creating a moment of stillness. The colors in the sky orange, pink, and purple are beautiful  and evoke a sense of wonder. Many people enjoy sunsets for their calming effect and the opportunity  to reflect or appreciate nature's beauty.
